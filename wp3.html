Genres of Literature

There are two main categories or genres of literature, fiction and nonfiction.

Genres of Fiction

Fiction derives from the author's imagination. Consider the following genres of fiction.

  • Drama
  • Mystery
  • Humor
  • Poetry
  • Science fiction
  • Fables
  • Fairy tales

Enjoy reading fiction.

Genres of Nonfiction

Nonfiction works are factual.

Enjoy reading nonfiction, such as Design and Development of Robust Instructional Apps. Consider the following genres of nonfiction.

  • essays
  • speeches
  • biographies
  • autobiographies
  • textbooks
