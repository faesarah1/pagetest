Genres of Literature

There are two main categories or genres of literature, fiction and nonfiction.

Genres of Fiction

Fiction derives from the author's imagination. Genres of fiction include drama, mystery, humor, poetry, science fiction, fables, and fairy tales, for instance.

Enjoy reading fiction.

Genres of Nonfiction

Nonfiction works are factual.

Enjoy reading nonfiction, such as Design and Development of Robust Instructional Apps. Genres of nonfiction include essays, speeches, biographies, autobiographies, and textbooks, for instance.
